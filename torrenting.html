Torrenting games: Torrenting major games is easy af. (only torrent single-player games)

 

Go to a torrent website, then search up the game you want to download. Find a link with a lot of seeders, and then click "Get this torrent"

 

 

Torrenting music/movies:

This process is the same as the one above, just go to a website, then search it up. If a movie has just been released it won't have a blue-ray copy, therefore all you'll find are versions where people recorded the movie with their phone or whatever in a movie theater. So just wait until the blue-ray comes out.

 

 

Torrenting books/audio books:

Here is where the process will differ a little. First off, you will often have trouble finding a book on a torrenting website.

Therefore, you'll probably google something like "book title epub/pdf download/torrent"

Obviously that will lead you to a bunch of shady af websites, but as long as you have the chrome extension "Web of Trust" you shouldn't get into a sticky situation :P

 

Obviously there are less audio books, but if you are Vincent and you like listening to audiobooks in your freetime, you might as well try to look for some.

 

 

 

Torrenting Applications:

Doing this is just the same process as torrenting a game. However the main catch in this area is that it is sometimes much harder to actually install the program.

Most of the time, when you have finished downloading a game, it will have a custom installer.

Meanwhile, after you have torrented a application, there oiten will be some shady stuff with a "Crack" or a "Keygen" or even editing the "Hosts File". Generally there aren't any mistakes when you install applications.
