OPP GUIDES

A short guide of what you want to do on Hereford Base.

 

Attacking the basement:

 

On the basement entrance you can shoot out the top part of the wall, giving a view of the lockers area.

Thatcher and Thermite should work together to open the locker area up, while the rest of the team clears the 3rd, 2nd, and first floor.

Thermite can use a charge into the armory wall, and Hibana can open up the 2 hatches into the basement.

 

 

Attacking first floor:

Thermite the Dining room and the Garage.

Have a person open up the front doors.

The other people can clear out the top 2 floors.
