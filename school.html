How are you supposed to prep for an AP test?

 

Generally, you should start prepping in March, as the test is in May. This gives you 10-9 weeks, or 2 months, to prepare. If you decided to study 20 minutes every day until the week of the test, you would easily get in 20 hours.

Be sure to take a bunch of practice tests to prepare you for how the final test will look like.
